About Us

The Bug Bounty Platform was established under the umbrella of Datamount, the first of its kind in Oman and the second in the Middle East.
It is an integrated platform that serves as an intermediary whose role is to manage and link security researchers in the field of cybersecurity to discover security vulnerabilities in the institutions' websites and programs with the aim of eliminating all potential security threats and raising the security level and ensure the rights of all parties.

Why BugBounty?

Bugbounty is a very powerful platform. Most of technology leader like google, Facebook, apple using crowdsourced security like bugbounty to reduce security risk:

• Like all forms of crowdsourcing, bugbounty security gathered a cybersecurity researcher (cyber attacker) to work in reducing or eliminating the risk of digital presences of the organization by vulnerabilities test. Bugbounty is crowdsources security platform (CSSP) which works as connection and management layer.

• Bugbounty gather skilled and experiences security researchers and ethical hackers. Also, will evaluate their skills and experiences and match it with test opportunities.

• Bugbounty will ensure smooth and fear vulnerability repot submission into the platform.

  • 01 Mission

    In Bug Bounty Oman, we strongly believe in building high cyber security and eliminating all potential digital threats to contribute in raising the technical level in the Sultanate of Oman.

  • 02 Vision

    To ensure a comprehensive and sustainable high level of cybersecurity in Oman through national talents of security researchers.

  • 03 Objective

    ⬡ The platform aims to raise the level of cyber security in the Sultanate by raising the security level of sites, programs and data.

    ⬡ The platform aims to link national competencies in the field of cybersecurity and institutions to exploit these competencies perfectly while ensuring complete confidentiality for security researchers as well as institutions.

    ⬡ To make the platform the first choice for all organizations that need innovative and diversified security research, to overcome security vulnerabilities, and to test the security capabilities of sites and programs

    ⬡ Train and coach national security researchers talented by promoting and motivating young people to exploit their creativity and talents in the field of cybersecurity.

    ⬡ Providing job opportunities for security researchers, aiming through the platform to be as a mediator between institutions and security researchers, in line with job opportunities in the field of cybersecurity.

 

How it works?

1

Registration

company or induvial must go through the registration process through the website (bugbounty.om) either as customer or as security researcher

2

Scope of work

customer must identify what asset need to be test it (website, web application, API, IOT, other). Customer must identify the enviroment test either in production or development or test enviroment. Customer will also identify the award amount depend on the critically of the finding (Critical, medium, low)

3

Researcher engagement

registered researcher will start applying to the new request from the customer and start the vulnerability assessment


4

Vulnerability submission

security researcher will upload their finding and automatically the platform will identify it either it is critical, Hight, Medium or Low finding

5

Reward

BugBounty team will validate the finding from each security researcher and either approval or reject the report. Based only on the approval of bugbounty and customer the reward will be given to the researcher after the bugbounty cut 10% of the award to manage the process
