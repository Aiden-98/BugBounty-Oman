Bug Bounty Oman

Established in 2021 under the umbrella of the Ministry of Transport, Communications, and Information Technology (MTC). It is the first of its kind in Oman and the second in the Middle East, which connects security researchers with enterprises. The security researchers shall be able to identify cyber threats and vulnerabilities and accordingly produce reports to the enterprises to implement gap closures plans.

About Us

Bug Bounty Oman platform aims to connect national talent platforms its of security researchers with state-owned and private-owned enterprises to discover and consequently eliminate potential threats to the security of cyber platforms.

Why us?

Utilizing competent local talents Under the theme innovation in Fintech and Cybersecurity, Oman National CERT We connects the right researchers to the right use cases and helps customers find, prioritize and fix security vulnerabilities at unprecedented speed.

  • 01 Mission

    In Bug Bounty Oman we strongly believe in building safer cybersecurity by proactively coordinating partnerships with researchers to make customers more secure. We utilize competent security researchers' talents to better protect billions of customers.

  • 02 Vision

    To ensure a comprehensive and sustainable high level of cybersecurity in Oman and worldwide, utilizing competent national talents of security researchers.

    Security
